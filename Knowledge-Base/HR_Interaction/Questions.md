# 📋 Complete Question List

## Personal Introduction

1. Tell me about yourself.
2. How would your colleagues describe you?
3. What are your strengths?
4. What are your weaknesses?
5. What are your hobbies and interests?
6. What motivates you?
7. What are your career goals?

---

## Company & Role

8. How did you hear about this opportunity?
9. Why do you want to work for TCS?
10. Why are you interested in this role?
11. What do you know about TCS?
12. Why should we hire you?
13. What value can you bring to our organization?

---

## Education & Background

14. Why did you choose your college?
15. Why did you choose your branch?
16. Tell us about your academic journey.
17. Which subjects did you enjoy the most?
18. Which subject did you find most challenging?

---

## Experience & Skills

19. How do you keep your technical skills up to date?
20. What programming languages are you comfortable with?
21. What technologies have you recently learned?
22. What development tools have you used?
23. What certifications have you completed?
24. What online resources do you use for learning?

---

## Projects

25. Describe your major project.
26. What technologies were used?
27. What challenges did you face?
28. How did you overcome those challenges?
29. What improvements would you make to the project?

---

## Behavioral Questions

30. Describe a challenging situation you faced.
31. Tell us about a time you worked under pressure.
32. Tell us about a failure and what you learned.
33. Describe your greatest achievement.
34. Describe a time you demonstrated leadership.
35. How do you handle conflicts within a team?
36. How do you prioritize multiple tasks?

---

## Workplace & Teamwork

37. Do you prefer working individually or in a team?
38. What qualities make a successful team?
39. Have you worked with software vendors or external teams?
40. How do you handle deadlines?
41. How do you ensure quality in your work?

---

## Technical Awareness

42. What technical websites or communities do you follow?
43. How do you explain technical concepts to non-technical people?
44. What is your troubleshooting approach?
45. Which software development methodology do you prefer?
46. What is your opinion on Agile methodology?

---

## Career & Professional Growth

47. Where do you see yourself in five years?
48. What kind of work environment do you prefer?
49. Are you willing to relocate?
50. Are you comfortable working in shifts?
51. How do you handle continuous learning?

---

## Situational Questions

52. How would you handle multiple deadlines?
53. How would you deal with project failure?
54. How would you resolve disagreements within your team?
55. How would you react if assigned an unfamiliar technology?
56. How would you explain a complex problem to a client?

---

## Closing Questions

57. Do you have any questions for us?
58. Is there anything else you would like us to know?
