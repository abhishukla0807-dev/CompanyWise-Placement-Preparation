# About Infosys

**Infosys Limited** is one of India's largest multinational IT services and consulting companies, headquartered in **Bengaluru, Karnataka**. It serves clients across more than **50 countries** in industries such as banking, financial services, insurance, retail, manufacturing, energy, and healthcare.

Infosys provides services in:

* Software Development
* Cloud Computing
* Artificial Intelligence
* Data Analytics
* Digital Transformation
* Cyber Security
* Enterprise Resource Planning
* Business Process Management
* Testing & Quality Assurance
* Infrastructure Management

For fresh graduates, Infosys is one of the largest campus recruiters in India and offers structured training and career development programs for new joiners.

# Eligibility Criteria

Eligibility may vary slightly depending on the hiring drive.

Typical requirements include:

* B.E. / B.Tech
* MCA
* M.Sc. (Computer Science / IT)
* M.Tech (Selected Drives)
* Minimum 60% or 6.0 CGPA throughout academics
* No active backlogs at the time of application
* Maximum 2 years of gap allowed in education
* Valid educational documents

# Preparation Strategy

A recommended preparation roadmap:

* Master Aptitude fundamentals
* Practice Logical Reasoning daily
* Improve English and Communication Skills
* Solve Coding Problems regularly
* Revise Core Computer Science subjects
* Build and understand your projects thoroughly
* Prepare a professional resume
* Practice mock interviews

Consistency is more important than studying for long hours occasionally.
