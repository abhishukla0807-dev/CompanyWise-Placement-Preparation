# 📖 HR Interview Overview

The HR interview is generally the final stage of the recruitment process.

Its purpose is to evaluate whether you are a good fit for the organization from a professional and behavioral perspective.

The interviewer focuses on your communication, attitude, confidence, and overall personality rather than technical knowledge.

---

# 🎯 Evaluation Criteria

Recruiters generally assess:

* Communication Skills
* Confidence
* Positive Attitude
* Professionalism
* Adaptability
* Teamwork
* Leadership Potential
* Problem-Solving Approach
* Learning Mindset
* Cultural Fit

# 📌 Interview Etiquette

## Before the Interview

* Research the company
* Revise your resume
* Test your setup (for virtual interviews)
* Dress professionally

## During the Interview

* Listen carefully
* Speak clearly
* Maintain eye contact
* Stay calm and composed

## After the Interview

* Thank the interviewer
* Exit politely
* Reflect on your performance
