# Overview

The **TCS Online Assessment (OA)** is the first stage of the recruitment process for most campus hiring programs such as:

* TCS NQT
* TCS Ninja
* TCS Prime
* TCS Digital

The assessment evaluates both aptitude and programming skills. Depending on the hiring drive, the number of sections and difficulty level may vary.

---

# Programming Tips

* Read the complete problem before coding.
* Handle all edge cases.
* Test using custom inputs.
* Avoid unnecessary nested loops.
* Prefer optimal solutions when possible.
* Keep variable names meaningful.
* Manage your time wisely.

---

# Common Mistakes

* Ignoring corner cases
* Integer overflow
* Incorrect input parsing
* Missing output format
* Not considering constraints
* Spending too much time on one question

# Frequently Asked Questions

### Is there negative marking?

Usually, no. However, always verify the instructions for your specific hiring drive.

---

### Is coding compulsory?

Yes. Coding performance plays an important role, especially for Prime and Digital hiring.

---

### Which language should I choose?

Use the language in which you are most comfortable. Accuracy is generally more important than using a specific language.

---

### What is the most important section?

There is no single most important section. Consistent performance across aptitude, reasoning, verbal ability, and coding significantly improves your chances of selection.
