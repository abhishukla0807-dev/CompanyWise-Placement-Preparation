## 📌 What Is This Repository?

This repository is a **one-stop hub** for engineering students, internship seekers, job aspirants, and exam candidates.  

It covers everything from **Online Assessments** to **HR Interviews**, with structured notes, problem solutions, and real experiences.  

Whether you are a **second-year student** exploring internships or a **final-year student** preparing for placements, this repo is designed to guide you step by step.

## 📚 Knowledge Base

Core Computer Science concepts organized for both **exam preparation** and **interview preparation**.

- **Aptitude** → Quant, reasoning, verbal ability.  
- **DSA** → Problems, solutions, techniques across arrays, strings, graphs, DP, trees.  
- **OOPS** → Four pillars, abstraction, inheritance, polymorphism, encapsulation.  
- **DBMS** → SQL, transactions, normalization, NoSQL.  
- **Operating System** → Concurrency, scheduling, deadlocks, memory management.  
- **Computer Networks** → OSI model, protocols, IP addressing, routing, security.  
- **System Design** → HLD, LLD, design patterns, case studies.  
- **AI/ML** → Algorithms, deep learning, NLP, MLOps.  
- **Languages** → Java, Python, C++ (with OOP centralized to avoid duplication).

### 🧭 How To Use the Internship Guide

1. **Start with How-To-Apply**  
   Learn the basics of finding opportunities, application strategies, and timelines.

2. **Polish your resume**  
   Use resume tips to highlight projects, skills, and achievements effectively.

3. **Reach out with Cold Email Template**  
   Send professional outreach emails to recruiters, mentors, or alumni.

4. **Choose your path based on your goal:**  
   - By **Company Type** → Product-Based, Service-Based, Startups.  
   - By **Domain** → Software Engineering (SWE), Data Science, Cybersecurity, DevOps, Product Management.  
   - By **Platform** → India, USA, Global, Remote.

5. **Understand stipends**  
   Get clarity on pay ranges, stipend expectations, and negotiation tips.

6. **Clarify doubts in FAQs**  
   Find answers to common questions about applications, interviews, and offers.

---

### 🌟 Why This Flow Works
- **Sequential guidance** → Students know exactly what to do first, next, and last.  
- **Flexible paths** → Choose by company type, domain, or platform depending on career goals.  
- **Practical resources** → Resume tips, cold emails, stipend guide make it actionable.  
- **Confidence boost** → FAQs and experiences reduce uncertainty and stress.
